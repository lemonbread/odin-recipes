Fries

Description

Potatoes came from the new world. Thus far the best forms of potatoes are fries (usually "chips" in the UK) and potato chips (or "crisps").

There is a legend that fries were tastier when they were commonly fried in beef tallow as opposed to vegetable oil. I am not a nutritionist, but for my recipe I will obey the tallow legend.

Proceed with caution: Fries are some of the tastiest foodstuffs known to man. Please consult a specialist before incorporating tallow fried fries into your diet permanently.

Ingredients

  • Potatoes

Steps

  1. Heat 1 & 1/2 to 2 sticks of beef tallow in a small pot.
  2. Portion your fries from your freezer into a small bowl.
  3. Wait about one minute after all of the tallow has melted.
  4. Carefully pour the fries into the pot. You may like to use a metal straining spoon(?) to guide them in.
  5. Wait about 7 minutes or until fries are hard and of your preferred colour.
  6. Patiently lift fries out of the pot with your straining spoon and place them into the bowl you used earlier.

  8. Dress with salt, chilli powder, pepper etc to taste. Also consider dips like mayo and/or hot sauce, ketchup, etc.
  9. Eat the fries.
